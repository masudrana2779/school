President Message

  • Release Date : 23 Oct 2020
  • President's Message

“As a man is, so is his speech.” Socrates. This is a universal truth for every human being. Human mind is ever creative and unique in expressing oneself. Human mind is expressed in and through speaking and writing. This is how the total human development takes place. Our main goals and objective is to educate the mind and heart of the students in the fullest. In order to be an integrated person and effective citizen, we offer and arrange various co-curricular activities. ‘This Institute’ is a platform where the students can express their mind and talents through creative writings. These writings witness how well they are growing along with the academic studies. Every year our Institute publishes the year book so that the students can cultivate their talents and express their creative vision.
